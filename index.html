NASA Internship

In the Fall of 2019, I was able to participate in a NASA internship at Johnson Space center. I worked with the Office of the Chief Information Officer under the Open Innovation Team, which manages how the public accesses NASA's public data and applications.

During this internship, I focused on three projects to aid the team. I worked on redesigning two of our offical pages, developed a backup system for NASA's public facing GitHub group, and visualized the Open Innovation Team's Tagger Code as an AR 3D Visualization.
